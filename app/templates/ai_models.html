Heart Disease Detection

The heart disease detection model accurately diagnoses patients with 100% recall on test data. The model is a fully connected neural network with 4 layers. The neural network has a skip connection from layer 2 to layer 4. The skip connection provides the model the ability to make deep and shallow connections and maintain the simple relationships between variables while also discover more complex relationships.
